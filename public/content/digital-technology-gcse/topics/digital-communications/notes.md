# Digital Communications

## Personal and social communication

- Digital communication methods have advantages, disadvantages and possible barriers.
- Personal and social communication methods include emailing, instant messaging, blogs, video conferencing and social networking websites/apps.
- Learners should understand social norms and expected behaviour when using them.

## Business communication

- Business communication methods may be internal or external.
- Examples include video conferencing, teleworking, emailing, promotion methods such as advertising, marketing and websites, and apps.

## Reliability of online sources

- Issues include accuracy of information, biased information and out of date information.
- Methods to verify online information include checking multiple sources and using trustworthy websites.

## Social networking practices and ownership

- Learners should understand how contemporary social networking platforms work.
- They should know benefits and drawbacks of social media.
- They should understand how social networking affects the concept of ownership of media.
