# Impact of Digital Systems on Organisations and Individuals

## Efficiencies and benefits

- Learners should identify benefits and drawbacks of office-based digital systems for organisations and consumer digital systems for individuals.

## Implementation methods

- Digital systems may be implemented using pilot, parallel or big bang methods.

## Changing working practices

- Learners should be aware of the impact changing working practices have had on culture and society.

## Changing relationships in commerce

- Learners should know advantages and disadvantages to customers and businesses of B2B EDI, online shopping, online marketplaces for third-party B2C or C2C sales, and B2B buying and selling.

## Rise of services and monetising content

- Learners should be aware of marketing to prospective and existing customers by email, social media or other methods.
- They should also understand advertising through websites, and data mining and analytics.
