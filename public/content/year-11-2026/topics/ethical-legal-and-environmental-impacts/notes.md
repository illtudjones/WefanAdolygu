# Ethical, Legal and Environmental Impacts

These revision notes are based on the WJEC GCSE Computer Science legacy specification (teaching from 2017 / award from 2019).

## Ethical impacts
- Digital technology raises ethical issues such as privacy and cybersecurity.
- Professionals should follow formal and informal codes of ethical behaviour.

## Legislation
- Current legislation affects security, privacy, data protection and freedom of information.

## Environmental issues
- Digital technology has environmental impacts, including energy use, e-waste and resource consumption.
